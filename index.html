About

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the DataNET Group at the College of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence, Fudan University, advised by Prof. Yang Chen and Prof. Xin Wang. I received my B.Eng. degree in Water Supply and Drainage Science and Engineering from the School of Environmental Science and Engineering, Tongji University, in 2024.
Recently, I have been working on LLM agents, with a focus on agent post-training and long-horizon reasoning, in close collaboration with Xinming Wang. If you are interested in chatting with me, feel free to drop me an email.
